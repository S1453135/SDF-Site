Welcome to the farm!

Storm Dancer Farm sits near Smithville Lake, just north of Kansas City MO. We raise pasture-based beef and dairy and meat goats - and hopefully poultry again soon!. Our animals are given as much opportunity as we can offer to graze or browse, with hay and some grain supplemented.

We are an affiliate of Goats On The Go® and operate in the greater KC area as Goats On The Go - Kansas City North&. Let us know if you're interested in having our crew of hungry goats come graze your troublesome weeds!

We welcome visitors to the farm to meet the animals. We travel too - we're more than happy to bring some four-legged friends if you would like a "meet and greet", educational event, or some goat yoga!
